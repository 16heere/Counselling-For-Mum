About Me

FAQ's

What is Therapy?

Therapy can help you cope with many difficult problems in life. You may have had a traumatic experience or event, or you may be feeling very low or anxious, especially in the current economy, or you may have developed harmful behaviours. Therapy can be very effective with all these problems and more.

What can I expect during a therapy session?

During a therapy session, you can expect a confidential and supportive environment where you can openly discuss your thoughts and feelings with a trained therapist. Together, you'll work towards understanding your challenges and developing strategies for positive change.

Is therapy confidential?

In therapy, confidentiality is a fundamental principle. It means that everything you discuss with your therapist remains private and protected. Your therapist is legally and ethically bound to keep your information confidential, except in certain specific situations where they are obligated to report, such as if there is a risk of harm to yourself or others.
